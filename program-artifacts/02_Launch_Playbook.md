# Launch Playbook — Last Mile Delivery Station

Standardized 9-phase playbook applied to every site launch. Each phase has entry criteria (what must be true to start), exit criteria / quality gate (what must be true to move on), owning department, key deliverables, and dependencies on other phases.

---

## Phase 1 — Site Planning
**Owner:** Operations (Launch Manager accountable)
**Duration:** ~T-120 to T-108 days

- **Activities:** Finalize site layout, confirm lease/permits, approve headcount plan, publish launch schedule to all stakeholders
- **Deliverables:** Approved site layout, published launch schedule, headcount plan
- **Entry criteria:** Lease signed, capital budget approved
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** Launch schedule published and acknowledged by all department leads; headcount plan approved by Finance
- **Dependencies:** None (program start)

## Phase 2 — Facilities Readiness
**Owner:** Facilities
**Duration:** ~T-108 to T-96 days

- **Activities:** Complete build-out, install racking/conveyor, dock leveler install, fire inspection
- **Deliverables:** Fire inspection sign-off, completed build-out walkthrough
- **Entry criteria:** Site Planning exit criteria met
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** Fire inspection passed; facility walkthrough signed off by Facilities Manager and Safety Manager
- **Dependencies:** Site Planning

## Phase 3 — Technology Readiness
**Owner:** Technology
**Duration:** ~T-96 to T-84 days

- **Activities:** Network/WiFi install, scanner provisioning, WMS go-live configuration, badge access system activation
- **Deliverables:** Network validation report, provisioned scanner fleet, live WMS instance
- **Entry criteria:** Facilities build-out substantially complete (power, racking in place)
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** WiFi coverage validated across full floor plan; WMS passes integration test; badge system live
- **Dependencies:** Facilities Readiness

## Phase 4 — Hiring Readiness
**Owner:** HR
**Duration:** ~T-96 to T-40 days (runs parallel to Technology)

- **Activities:** Post job requisitions, run hiring events, complete background checks, track offer acceptance against target
- **Deliverables:** Hiring completion report, background check clearance log
- **Entry criteria:** Headcount plan approved
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** ≥ 90% of headcount target hired and background-check cleared
- **Dependencies:** Site Planning

## Phase 5 — Training Readiness
**Owner:** Training
**Duration:** ~T-40 to T-14 days

- **Activities:** Trainer certification, new-hire orientation scheduling, SOP and safety training delivery
- **Deliverables:** Training completion report, trainer certification log
- **Entry criteria:** ≥ 75% of headcount hired (Hiring Readiness substantially complete)
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** ≥ 95% of Day 1 associates trained and certified
- **Dependencies:** Hiring Readiness

## Phase 6 — Safety Validation
**Owner:** Safety
**Duration:** ~T-24 to T-14 days

- **Activities:** Safety audit, PPE stock confirmation, emergency evacuation plan posting, incident escalation process test
- **Deliverables:** Passed safety audit, posted evacuation plan
- **Entry criteria:** Facilities Readiness exit criteria met
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** Safety audit passed with zero open critical findings
- **Dependencies:** Facilities Readiness

## Phase 7 — Operational Testing
**Owner:** Operations
**Duration:** ~T-14 to T-7 days

- **Activities:** Scanner load test, route staging dry run, dock throughput test, peak volume simulation
- **Deliverables:** Operational test report
- **Entry criteria:** Technology Readiness and Safety Validation exit criteria met
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** Dock throughput and route staging tests meet target volume with zero critical defects
- **Dependencies:** Technology Readiness, Safety Validation, Training Readiness (associates needed for dry runs)

## Phase 8 — Day 1 Launch
**Owner:** Operations (Site Leader accountable on-site)
**Duration:** T-7 days through Launch Day

- **Activities:** Go/No-Go review, leadership sign-off, Day 1 staffing confirmation, launch war room staffed
- **Deliverables:** Go/No-Go decision record, Day 1 Readiness Checklist (see artifact 07)
- **Entry criteria:** All prior phases at exit criteria; Day 1 Readiness Checklist 100% pass
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** Formal Go decision issued by Launch Manager + Regional Operations Director
- **Dependencies:** All prior phases

## Phase 9 — Post-Launch Stabilization
**Owner:** Operations
**Duration:** Day 1 through T+30 days

- **Activities:** Daily stand-ups (Week 1), issue log review and resolution, 30-day performance review, lessons learned session
- **Deliverables:** Post-Launch Retrospective (see artifact 08)
- **Entry criteria:** Day 1 launch complete
- **Exit criteria / quality gate:** All Day 1 critical issues resolved; retrospective completed and filed
- **Dependencies:** Day 1 Launch

---

## Standardization Note

Every phase produces the same artifact types at every site — this is what makes the launch process repeatable rather than reinvented per site. The Site Readiness Scorecard rolls these 9 phases up into a single readiness score per site so the program can be tracked consistently regardless of region, size, or launch date.
